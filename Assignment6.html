Why climbing is a great hobby

Bouldering is so much fun and an enjoyable challenge! Each route feels like a new puzzle, and there's something so satisfying about finally reaching the top after so many attempts. Plus, the vibe at climbing gym is top tier makes it even more enjoyable! Check out a great climbing gym in Boulder, Colorado called The Spot. Another great place to climb (especially if you're a beginner and a student) is right here at the University of Colorado Boulder.
